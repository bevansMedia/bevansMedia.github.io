About me

Hello there

A little bit about myself

I've been playing games for basically all of my life and have always been interested in developing them, starting with creating scenarios in Age of Empires 2, making Counter-Strike maps in the Source Engine's editor Hammer, before moving onto using Unity professionaly.

I originally wanted to be an game artist but ended up primarily programming, in C#. Working on my own projects has also allowed me a chance to learn modelling, texturing, animation and UI design.

I have worked on many projects both in teams and individually, for both Desktop and Mobile devices. I mainly work remotely, but had the oppertunity to spend 4 months in sunny Sydney to work alongside the directors of Nosy Jones on the Chicken Pox project.

When I'm not working, I'm usually found playing drums or learning guitar, playing video or board games or watching Star Trek.
