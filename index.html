About me

A little bit about myself

I have been interested by game developement for most of my life, starting with creating scenarios in Age of Empires 2, making Counter-Strike maps in Hammer and moving onto using Unity professionaly.

I originally wanted to be an artist but ended up primarily programming in C#. Working on my own projects has given me a chance to scratch the creative itch, allowing to learn modelling, texturing, animation and UI design.

I have worked on many projects both in teams and individually, for both Desktop and Mobile devices.
